Every out-of-stock product page is a fork in the road. Shoppers either leave forever — or they leave their email and come back when you restock.

## The hidden cost of stockouts

When a bestseller sells out, merchants often celebrate the demand. But without a capture mechanism, that demand evaporates. The customer who wanted your product may buy from a competitor instead.

Back-in-stock notifications solve this by:

1. **Capturing intent** while the shopper is still on your product page
2. **Automating outreach** the moment inventory returns
3. **Building a demand signal** you can use for purchasing decisions

## What makes a good notify-me flow

Not all back-in-stock tools are equal. Merchants should look for:

- A **theme-native widget** that matches the storefront — collapsed to a simple "Notify me when available" bar, expandable to a full signup form
- **Reliable email delivery** when stock changes
- A **subscriber dashboard** to see what customers want
- **Simple setup** — minutes, not days
